# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP): "Ocenka Group"

## 1. Global Audience Context
Users visiting this website are not browsing for entertainment. They have a strict, often urgent, financial or legal necessity (e.g., mortgage approval, court dispute, business sale). They are stressed, value their time, and are looking for a reliable expert to delegate their problem to entirely.

## 2. B2B Segment (Corporate Clients - High Ticket)
* **Who they are:** Business owners, CEOs, Chief Accountants, Corporate Lawyers, Investors.
* **Why they need us:** Valuation of business shares, commercial real estate, industrial equipment, or intellectual property for bank loans, M&A deals, taxation, or bankruptcy.
* **Pain Points:** * Bureaucracy and missed deadlines.
  * Incompetent reports that banks or courts reject.
  * Wasting time explaining basic things to the contractor.
* **What they value:** Speed, absolute confidentiality, legal bulletproofness, and a premium, "no-nonsense" approach. They are willing to pay a premium price for a clean, hassle-free result.

## 3. B2C Segment (Individual Clients - High Volume)
* **Who they are:** Homebuyers, heirs, individuals going through a divorce or property dispute, car owners.
* **Why they need us:** Mortgage valuation for banks, inheritance paperwork for notaries, assessing damage after an accident or flooding.
* **Pain Points:**
  * Confused by legal jargon and complex procedures.
  * Afraid of hidden fees or overpaying.
  * Anxious about their documents being rejected by the bank or notary.
* **What they value:** Transparency (clear pricing), step-by-step guidance, and a fast, predictable process (hence the need for an interactive Quiz).

## 4. User Intent & Psychological Triggers
* **Trust is paramount:** The premium design of the website must instantly signal that this company is a high-end, reliable entity.
* **The "Done for You" mindset:** The user wants to leave their phone number and hear: "We have taken care of everything."
* **Instant Gratification:** Users want to know the approximate cost and timeframe immediately. The interactive Quiz satisfies this trigger perfectly.

## 5. Copywriting & UX Guidelines for AI Agent
* **Tone of Voice:** Authoritative, reassuring, professional, and concise. 
* **Rule:** Do not sell "valuation services". Sell the "resolution of a legal/financial problem."
* **UX Implication:** Keep forms incredibly simple. Ask only what is necessary (Name, Phone, Property Type). Let the sales manager handle the complex questions during the callback.
* **Language:** Use clear, precise business Russian for the final website copy, avoiding cheap marketing gimmicks.

Project Plan: Ocenka Group Website Redesign

Recommended Direction
Use a conservative premium corporate style typical of strong legal, valuation, and financial firms in Russia.

This means:
- light background
- dark navy text
- restrained gold accent
- strong typography
- large whitespace
- clean document-like layouts
- minimal motion
- no flashy startup effects
- no experimental art direction

This is the best fit because the goal is sales, and buyers need trust, speed, and legal confidence more than originality.

Positioning
The homepage should present the company as a federal valuation partner for private clients and business.

Main message:
"Оценка имущества по всей России. Отчеты для банка, суда, нотариуса и бизнеса."

Support message:
"Квартиры, дома, коммерческая недвижимость, бизнес, ущерб, оборудование и транспорт. Сразу называем стоимость, сроки и список документов."

Best Homepage Strategy
Do not make one generic "about company" page.

Make one conversion homepage with immediate routing:
1. Частным клиентам
2. Бизнесу

Then route into focused landing pages.

Design System
Recommended palette:
- background: #F7F5F0
- surface: #FFFFFF
- primary text: #1F2A37
- muted text: #5B6472
- accent: #B68A45
- border: #E7E1D6

Typography:
- headings: elegant serif or strong business serif
- body: neutral sans-serif
- tone: legal-financial, calm, precise

Visual behavior:
- subtle card lift
- soft fade-in on scroll
- very restrained gold highlights on CTA and trust markers
- premium document-like blocks
- no aggressive shadows

Conversion Rules
1. One main CTA repeated across page.
2. One short universal form on homepage.
3. Quiz is the main lead capture element.
4. Long explanations move to service pages.
5. Trust proof must appear before deep copy.
6. Mobile click-to-call must stay visible.

Asset Use Plan
From the existing files:
- certificates go into trust/documents section
- PDFs become downloadable proof examples
- feedback screenshots become review wall / carousel
- company.png and news.png support the social proof block

Key Copy Direction
Do not sell "valuation" as a service by itself.
Sell outcomes:
- report accepted
- clear сроки
- понятная стоимость
- минимум действий со стороны клиента
- legal and business confidence

Best Strategic Choice
The homepage should be broad enough for all-country traffic, but structured so each visitor sees their path in the first screen or second block.

That gives:
- better paid traffic conversion
- better SEO landing expansion
- clearer user journey
- stronger trust perception
